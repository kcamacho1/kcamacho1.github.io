Internship Program

At Claymont, a nonprofit nature preserve and retreat center, I created the organizing structure for their first permaculture internship program. I surveyed community members to identify a consistent pain point — lack of organizing clarity — which was impeding collaboration.

Using my background in network engineering, I adapted the concept of star topology to reframe the team hierarchy. Once presented, the structure received majority buy-in and enabled the successful launch and completion of the internship.
