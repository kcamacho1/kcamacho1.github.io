Internship Program

At Claymont (A Non-Profit Nature Preserve and Retreat Center) I created the organizing structure for the Claymont Retreat Centers first permaculture intership program. Before arriving there was discourse within the community members however after surveying the individuals I was able to recognize a consistent pain point (heirarchy structure) that was hindering progress of this program.

Using experience from network engineering I used star topology as inspiration for the new organizing structure. After presenting this information to the community members we had majority buy in and was able to successfully kick off and complete the permaculture program at Claymont!
