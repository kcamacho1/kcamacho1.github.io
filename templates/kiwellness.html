Overview

Ki Wellness is an AI-powered holistic health platform that combines food journaling, macro tracking, personalized meal suggestions, exercise playlists, and spiritual coaching — all in one beautifully integrated app. It is designed to help users maintain physical, mental, and spiritual well-being through intelligent, data-driven recommendations.

My Role & Responsibilities

  • Designed and built the frontend using Next.js & TailwindCSS
  • Developed backend APIs with FastAPI & Python
  • Integrated Supabase for authentication and database management
  • Connected third-party APIs (Nutritionix, OpenFoodFacts) for nutrition data
  • Implemented AI meal and wellness suggestions with OpenAI
  • Set up deployment and hosting on Vercel & Render

Key Features

  • Food Journal: Users log meals and get real-time macro breakdowns
  • AI Recommendations: Personalized meal, workout, and mindfulness suggestions
  • Smart Database: Tracks user progress and customizes recommendations
  • Exercise Playlist: Curated weekly workout routines
  • Spiritual Guidance: AI-powered wellness prompts and daily reflections
