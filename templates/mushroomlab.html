MycoCulture Mushroom Lab

Edible Mushroom Lab & Sustainable Agriculture Project

Role: Project Coordinator, Builder, Proposal Writer, Strategist, Head Producer

Overview

I proposed, designed, and built a low-cost edible mushroom lab with a clean room and laminar flow system for a permaculture nonprofit. The lab supports spore cultivation, inoculation, and fruiting of mushrooms like Shiitake, Lion’s Mane, and Turkey Tail.

Specifications & Goals

  • Grow and distribute organic edible mushrooms to local markets
  • Hire and train community members for harvesting, cleaning, and distribution
  • Study companion planting between mushrooms and trees for long-term food forests
