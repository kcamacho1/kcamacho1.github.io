MycoCulture

Edible mushrooms for sustainable living

| Shiitaki | Lions Mane | Turkey Tail |

I drafted a business proposal to build an edible mushroom lab for a non-profit permaculture center and had it approved by the board of directors. Once approved I designed and built an edible mushroom lab with a clean room and lamnar flow system on a tight budget. This allowed for spore and mycelium storage, innoculation and fruiting.

Specifications

  • To Grow Edible Organic Mushrooms to sell to Market
  • To employ Mushroom Harvesters, People to Clean/ Prepare Mushrooms to be Market Ready and Sellers or Distributors for Market
  • To Study and Research (Companion Planting) the symbiotic relationship between Mushrooms and Plants to create a recurring annual Mushroom Harvest.

  • To grow and store Medicinal Mushrooms for Claymont residential and apothecary use
