Tank Bot

Self driving RC car with Arduino board written in C++ with Collision Detection. Features include sound module, LED lights and recoil effects

Quick Specs

  • Consistant Speed (1mps)
  • Drive in a Straight Line (DC Motors)
  • 90 degree Right turn
  • Durable
  • Turret Swivel on servo motor
  • Reverse
  • Ultrasonic sensor
  • Recoil (C++ Programming)
  • Tank Hatch
  • Modular components (Bambu 3D printer)
  • Shooting Sound (Sound Module)
  • LED Light
  • Board: Arduino Uno REV3 [A000066]
  • Design: InventorCAD
